About NWSDB

The National Water Supply & Drainage Board is the principal authority providing safe drinking water and facilitating sanitation services in Sri Lanka.

Our Vision

To be the most prestigious water utility in South Asia.

Our Mission

We serve the nation by providing sustainable water and sanitation solutions, ensuring total customer satisfaction.
